ABOUT THIS RESEARCH

This research is being conducted by Bruno Cartaxo (PhD), Gustavo Pinto (PhD), Márcio Ribeiro (PhD), Maria Teresa Baldassarre (PhD), and Sergio Soares (PhD).

We are researchers at UFPE (Federal University of Pernambuco, Brazil), UFPA (Federal University of Para, Brazil), UFAL (Federal University of Alagoas, Brazil)and UNIBARI (Univeristy of Bari, Italy) studying methods to transfer knowledge to software engineering practice.

One way to do this, is to make systematic reviews more connected to practitioners' needs. To achieve this goal, we are planning to borrow the idea of Rapid Reviews from Medicine, but before, we would like to know your opinion about the use of Rapid Reviews in software engineering.

The estimated time to complete the questionnaire is 45 minutes.

If you have any doubt, do not hesitate to contact us in bfsc@cin.ufpe.br and/or visit our website on http://cin.ufpe.br/eseg
